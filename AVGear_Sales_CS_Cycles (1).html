Handoff point — Sales → Client Success
The Client Success cycle begins when physical inventory arrives at the AVGear warehouse. The Sales Associate's active ownership of the client relationship ends at Stage 8 above. The Director of Client Success takes full ownership from inventory receipt forward. A formal CS Briefing (deal type, terms, client expectations, any commitments made during the sale) must be completed in HubSpot before the handoff is considered complete.gap to define
